    Optimizing material flow in a multi – echelon, multi – mode, multi – product Supply Chain Network.

  • This project was undertaken in requirement as a part of the internship under the guidance of Dr. Subhas C. Misra at IIT – Kanpur.
  • The project aimed to optimize material flow in a multi – echelon, multi – mode, multi – product Supply Chain Network by optimizing the distribution and transportation costs associated with the supply and demand.
  • The heuristic followed was a 2 stage process which involved finding the basic feasible solution for the distribution costs using VAM and optimizing the basic feasible solution using a method of multipliers.
  • The transportation costs were found using Gurobi Optimizer as the VSP reduces to a single-constrained knapsack problem.
